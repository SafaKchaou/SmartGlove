=======================================
 SMART GLOVE — BREADBOARD WIRING NOTES
=======================================

PIC16F877A Power, Reset & Crystal:
- Pin 11 (VDD) → 5V rail
- Pin 32 (VDD) → 5V rail
- Pin 12 (VSS) → GND rail
- Pin 31 (VSS) → GND rail
- Pin 1  (MCLR) → 10kΩ → 5V rail
- Pin 13 (OSC1) → crystal leg 1 + 22pF cap to GND
- Pin 14 (OSC2) → crystal leg 2 + 22pF cap to GND

LCD (16x2, 4-bit parallel):
- LCD pin 1  (VSS) → GND
- LCD pin 2  (VDD) → 5V
- LCD pin 3  (V0)  → POT wiper (POT ends: 5V and GND)
- LCD pin 4  (RS)  → PIC RD0 (Pin 19)
- LCD pin 5  (RW)  → GND
- LCD pin 6  (EN)  → PIC RD1 (Pin 20)
- LCD pins 7-10    → not connected
- LCD pin 11 (D4)  → PIC RD4 (Pin 27)
- LCD pin 12 (D5)  → PIC RD5 (Pin 28)
- LCD pin 13 (D6)  → PIC RD6 (Pin 29)
- LCD pin 14 (D7)  → PIC RD7 (Pin 30)
- LCD pin 15 (A)   → 5V
- LCD pin 16 (K)   → GND

MPU6050:
- VCC   → 3.3V rail (separate supply)
- GND   → GND rail (common)
- SDA   → PIC RC4 (Pin 23) + 10kΩ pull-up to 3.3V
- SCL   → PIC RC3 (Pin 18) + 10kΩ pull-up to 3.3V
- AD0   → GND (address 0x68)
- FSYNC → GND
- INT   → not connected

Traffic Light Module:
- GND → GND rail
- R   → PIC RB0 (Pin 33)
- Y   → PIC RB1 (Pin 34)
- G   → PIC RB2 (Pin 35)

ESP32-CAM:
- 5V  → 5V rail
- GND → GND rail
- GPIO 13 → voltage divider junction

Voltage Divider (PIC TX 5V → 3.3V for ESP32):
- PIC RC6/TX (Pin 25) → 10kΩ → junction
- Junction → 10kΩ → 10kΩ → GND (two 10kΩ in series = 20kΩ)
- Junction → wire → ESP32-CAM GPIO 13
